About Become One!

Wonderful things happen in our daily life! We always look forward to a wonderful day, but unfortunately things happen outside our control. Everyday, there are millions of people who struggle with and suffer from diseases, illnesses and addictions. A cancer patient going through chemotherapy. A patient with renal failure going through dialysis. A patient suffering from apositia, anorexia or bulimia. Even the daily smoker has their life altered by their addiction. All of these are afflictions evil! Although we do our best to not let these ailments control our life, they sometimes can take the day away from you.

It is time for us to fight for our life! Sometimes, due to these illnesses, it may seem impossible for people to have a better day, but we believe that the day is only what you make it. We choose to make delicious healthy food. A healthy daily diet is always a good friend and is usually the first step in helping to cure these sicknesses. Our goal is to provide these people, who were trapped in the bad situation, with a quick, wonderful and healthy daily diet recipes, according to their troubles. Our target audience are people with illnesses that can have positive outcomes from a good diet. For example, if a person is suffering from diabetes, on our website, he or she can get the legitimate daily diet that will tailored for people with diabetes that is easy to make. Now, he or she can spend their time doing what they want, and not what the diabetes tells them to.
